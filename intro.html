“Strategy without tactics is the slowest route to victory. Tactics without strategy is the noise before defeat.”

- Sun Tzu, The Art of War

The terms tactic and strategy are often confused: tactics are the actual means used to gain an objective, while strategy is the overall campaign plan.
  • strategy - what is your objective
  • tactics - how will you achieve your objective?

Best Talks!!

  • What elements did the presentation have that you liked?
  • What techniques did the speaker use that sparked interest?
  • How did you feel during/after the talk?
  • What impressions did you get about the speaker during the talk?
  • What impressions did you get about the content during the talk?

Other Talks!!

  • What elements of the presentation did you dislike?
  • What techniques did the speaker use that were potentially irksome?
  • How did you feel during/after the talk?
  • What impressions did you get about the speaker during the talk?
  • What impressions did you get about the content during the talk?
